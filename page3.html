Maroon 5

Maroon 5 is a band formed in 1994 consisting of the Adam Levine (Lead Singer), Jesse Carmicheal (Rythmn Guitarist & Keyboardist), James Valentine (Lead Guitarist), Matt Flynn (Drummer), PJ Morton (Keyboardist), and Sam Farrar (Multi-Instrumentalist & Bassist). The band would start to gain popularity and release more albums, My favourites being "V", "Songs About Jane", "Jordi", and "Overexposed". Maroon 5 plays a wide range of genres which include Pop, Pop Rock, Funk Rock, Blue-eyed soul, Dance-pop, and Soft Rock. One of my favorite songs by maroon 5 are Sugar, This Love, Payphone, She will be loved, Animals, Sunday Morning, and Cold.
